The Best Movies According To Lukesh

My top 5 movies of all time

1. Bahubali : The Beginning

It is an epic Indian film that tells the story of a heroic prince seeking to reclaim his kingdom, featuring stunning visuals and grandiose action sequences.

2. The Avengers

T "Avengers" is a blockbuster superhero ensemble film where Earth's mightiest heroes unite to save the world from powerful villains and cosmic threats

3. KGF 2

"KGF Chapter 2" is an action-packed Indian film that continues the saga of a young man's rise to power in the ruthless world of gold mining.

4. Pushpa

"Pushpa" is a Telugu action thriller that follows the life of a smuggler as he navigates the dangerous world of red sandalwood smuggling in the forests of Andhra Pradesh.

5.Hera Fari

"Hera Pheri" is a classic Bollywood comedy that revolves around the hilarious misadventures of three individuals who get entangled in a web of mistaken identities and financial troubles.
